Our Introduction

Vel Tech Boiler System
— Chennai

Vel Tech Boiler System (VTBS) is a Chennai-based industrial engineering firm specialising in the complete lifecycle of boiler systems. We provide expert erection, commissioning, servicing, and full annual maintenance of steam boilers, thermic fluid heaters, and boiler pipeline systems. With over 15 years of hands-on site experience and 200+ completed projects, VTBS is the trusted single-point partner for industries across Tamil Nadu that demand zero downtime and maximum boiler efficiency.

What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive boiler engineering solutions — from first installation to lifetime maintenance, all under one roof.

01 — Service
🔧

Boiler Erection & Commissioning

Expert installation and commissioning of fire tube, water tube, and package boilers with precision alignment and stage-by-stage safety verifications as per IBR.

02 — Service
♨️

Steam Boiler Service & Repair

Scheduled preventive maintenance and rapid emergency repair services for all steam boilers — ensuring optimal performance and uninterrupted production.

03 — Service
🌡️

Thermic Fluid Heater Systems

Supply, erection, commissioning, and full servicing of thermic fluid (hot oil) heating systems for textile, chemical, food, and pharma industries.

04 — Service
🏗️

Turnkey Fabrication & Erection

End-to-end turnkey contracts — structural fabrication, boiler room construction, platform erection, and all ancillary equipment on a single-responsibility basis.

05 — Service
🔩

Boiler Pipeline Work (IBR)

Design, fabrication, and IBR-certified installation of steam lines, feed-water piping, blow-down systems, and all fittings, flanges, and valves.

06 — Service
💧

Pump Service & Insulation Works

Overhauling of feed pumps and condensate pumps; plus professional thermal insulation of boiler bodies, steam pipes, and vessels to minimise heat loss.

Industries We Power

Our boiler solutions deliver reliable thermal energy across a wide range of industrial sectors throughout Tamil Nadu and South India.

Textile & Garments

Steam supply for dyeing, drying, and calendering in textile mills.

Food & Beverage
